Digital Sustainability Consultant (DSC)

Learning Pathway Position

Foundation level in Level 6 Diploma in Digital Sustainability Consultancy
This curriculum prepares learners to provide professional consulting services and advisory support for digital sustainability transformations in diverse organisational contexts. Graduates will develop expertise in sustainability assessment methodologies, solution design frameworks, and client engagement strategies. They typically work with SMEs and mid-sized organisations to design and implement sustainability strategies, providing expert guidance on digital tools and methodologies that support environmental objectives.
