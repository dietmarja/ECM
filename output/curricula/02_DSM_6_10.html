Digital Sustainability Manager (DSM)

Learning Pathway Position

Entry learning unit in Level 6 Diploma in Digital Sustainability Management
This curriculum prepares learners to understand core principles of digital sustainability and develop fundamental management skills for coordinating sustainability initiatives across organisational functions. Graduates will master essential frameworks for implementing sustainability strategies, coordinating cross-functional teams, and translating sustainability objectives into operational processes. They typically manage sustainability implementation projects and serve as key coordinators between technical teams and strategic leadership.
